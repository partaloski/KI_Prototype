How do I get a certificate?

  • In order to get a certificate, you need to partake in all of the exams that will be posted on the course whenever there are enough students for there to be an exam scheduled, the next exam is estimated to be around the 15th of May, 2022.

    • What kinds of questions should I expect on the exams?

  • The questions on the exams always represent what was taught on the videos, the questions that you'll most likely find on the exams will be either multiple choice or essay questions if the question can be answered with a text from the student, some questions that were on previous exams can be found here.

    • Who can take the exam?

  • Everyone can! You just need to be logged in and enrolled into the course!

    • What happens when I pass the exam?

  • You will be contacted on the e-mail address that's connected to your account, so make sure you can log in to that specific address before taking an exam. That's where you'll get the results.
